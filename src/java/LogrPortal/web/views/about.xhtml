About Logr

Logr serves as an essential logbook application within ASD APS, flexibly caters to to the specific requirements of various technical groups, including AOP, CTL, and MCR OPS. Its primary purpose is to facilitate the organized recording of logbook entries, with an emphasis on chronological organization and the ability to group entries by task or shift. This approach accommodates the needs of technical group members making occasional entries and operational or physics crews creating a sequence of grouped entries for shifts or studies. Logr offers exceptional flexibility by allowing users to define their own properties, tailoring the logbook to suit their specific requirements. Additionally, it provides robust search features, enhancing the efficiency of logbook entry retrieval and analysis.
